"About Us

Who we are…

Cinnamon, previously known as Universal Caterers is recogised as one of the leading caterers in the Delhi region owing primarily to maticulous planning, faultless execution and an unflinching attention to Detail". Pawan Anand, the founder of Anand Caterers has been the guiding force and inspiration since 1984. Under his guidance, Universal Caterers has captured the imagination of Delhiites by weaving its magic on every occasion. Starting way back in 1984, we have come a long way and emerged as one of the preferred Indian food caterers in the whole of Delhi and NCR. Our expertise is unmatched and today we are a name to reckon with when it comes to catering for events and weddings in Delhi.
